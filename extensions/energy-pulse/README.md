# Energy Pulse ⚡

**Intelligent Power & Battery Management for PCLink**

Energy Pulse is a theme-aware power management extension for PCLink that provides real-time monitoring and control of your node's energy profiles.

## Features

- **Live Status**: Monitor battery percentage and AC power status.
- **Power Plans**: Switch between `Saver`, `Balanced` (Auto), and `High` (Turbo) performance modes.
- **Dynamic Widget**: Compact dashboard widget showing battery health and current plan.
