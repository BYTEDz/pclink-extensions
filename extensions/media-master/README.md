# Media Master 🎵

**Remote Media & Playback Control for PCLink**

Media Master allows you to control the playback and volume of your node from your PCLink dashboard.

## Features

- **Playback Control**: Play/Pause, Next, and Previous track support.
- **Volume Management**: Precise volume increment, decrement, and mute controls.
- **Dynamic Widget**: Floating playback and volume panel for your dashboard.
